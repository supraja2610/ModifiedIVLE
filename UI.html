CP3101B

Web Programming and Applications



Inroduction


I (Dr Steven Halim) have the experience of creating a (simple and static) personal website since year 2000 and the highly successful VisuAlgo: algorithm visualization and animation website since 2011. However, I am still learning the latest web development techniques as of year 2014-2015. In Semester 2 (Jan-Apr 2015) of this Academic Year (AY) 2014/15, I am assigned to teach this new module CP3101B - Web Programming and Applications Show the official module description with Steven's improvisation. This revamped "World of Seven" website and in particular this single -- all-in "cp3101b.html" webpage will be the main page of CP3101B*. I started this page on Monday, 30 June 2014 and since then I gradually update this page with the contents of my version of CP3101B. The starting version (of my version) of CP3101B is the one created by Arnold Rosenbloom, a visiting lecturer from University of Toronto, Canada who taught the first edition of CP3101B in NUS in Jan-Apr 2014. Due to different background and expertise, my version will be necessarily different from Arnold's version. If you are a current/prospective CP3101B student in Semester 2 of AY2014/2015, please bookmark this webpage and occassionally check for updates :).
